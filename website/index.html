Key Principles of the Project

Principle 1: The Biological Basis

Your point of origin is encoded in the Genome. We view it not simply as a 'blueprint for the body,' but as a primordial mechanism for perception and interpretation. The Genome is a unique prism through which the Universe gains shape and meaning.

Principle 2: The Physical Basis

The Universe is defined not by objects, but by relationships. Reality is not a warehouse of pre-existing things. It is a dynamic web of interconnections that coalesces at the moment of observation. You are not observing objects—you are establishing the relationships that give rise to their perceived properties.

Principle 3: The Mechanism of Action

The perception of reality is an act of measurement, and every measurement requires a standard. The choice of a coordinate system, scale, and criteria for comparison is a fundamental act that transforms a field of potentiality into an ordered, observable Universe.
